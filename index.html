📄 Whitepaper

Everything you need to know about MCF

Monaco Capital Frank (MCF)

Abstract

MCF is a premium digital asset on the Solana blockchain, inspired by the financial heritage of Monaco. With a fixed supply of 10 billion tokens and a 0.25% transfer fee, MCF is designed for sustainable growth and long-term value.

📜 Historical Connection

The project is named after the historical Monegasque franc (MCF), which was pegged to the euro at a fixed rate of 1 EUR = 6.55957 MCF. This historical connection gives MCF a unique identity in the crypto space.

🌟 Vision

To bridge traditional finance and decentralized technology, offering users a secure, transparent, and fast payment solution. With integrations planned with Monte Carlo casinos, yacht clubs, and luxury real estate, MCF aims to become the digital currency of the Riviera.
